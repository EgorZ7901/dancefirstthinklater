About Me

Dance First Think Later is a Vancouver-based company that specializes in providing great dance packages for soon-to-be-married couples who really want to shine when their first dance comes on. Sometimes being in the spotlight like on the day of your wedding and especially when the first song comes on, can be nerve wracking. The pros at DFTL will help ease your nerves so you can take the stage and show your moves!
Katya Petrytskyi is the owner and dance instructor extraordinaire leading Dance First Think Later. She started her dance training at the tender age of six and taking on a number of national and international ballroom dance competitions throughout her career. As a recent newlywed herself, she is now available to coach other couples for their long awaited wedding dance.
